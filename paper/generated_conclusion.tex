The gated hybrid improved its TimeMixer base at all five horizons and reduced
the severe over-correction of the ungated residual model. However, it won 0 of
25 comparisons against the strongest matched non-hybrid comparator and had
mean relative improvement of -23.33\% under that stricter reference. LSTM was
the best pooled model (MAE 3.271). The evidence supports calibration gating as
a useful safeguard, but it does not support overall superiority or a
state-of-the-art claim.
