The confirmatory experiment completed five stations, five seeds, and a 30-epoch maximum. The gated rolling hybrid won 0 of 25 station--horizon comparisons, with mean relative MAE improvement of -23.33\% against the strongest matched non-hybrid comparator. The lowest pooled MAE was 3.271, obtained by LSTM. A common-history sensitivity run restricted all stations to a 2021-start history and changed hybrid MAE by only \(+0.012\) on average, supporting the same qualitative conclusion. The result does not support a superiority or state-of-the-art claim.
