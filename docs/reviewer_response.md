# Reviewer feedback revision

## EDA

The nine individual EDA plots are no longer separate main-text figures.
`01_data_overview.png` and `02_data_quality.png` are the two composite EDA
figures. Detailed plots `S01`--`S09` are generated under
`figures/supplementary/`.

## Effectiveness and stations

The multi-station runner evaluates every station with the same chronology,
models, seeds, and horizons. The hybrid is compared with the strongest
non-hybrid comparator per station/horizon, not only with a weak baseline.
The completed run covers five stations and 25 station--seed fits. The hybrid
improves its TimeMixer base at every pooled horizon, but wins 0/25 comparisons
against the strongest matched comparator. The revision therefore demonstrates
effective residual stabilization, not overall superiority or SOTA performance.

## Residual layer

The revised stage includes calibration-median de-biasing, pseudo-Huber
XGBoost, calibration-derived correction clipping, and 30-day rolling
calibration. A target error becomes eligible only when its target timestamp is
no later than the current forecast origin.

## Robustness

The full runner is fixed to seeds 0--4 and 30 epochs. Quick/demo outputs must
not be reported.

## Unequal training-history sensitivity

HP1 has no coverage in 2016--2018, BIR_A4540 begins in 2021, CHB has reduced
coverage in 2024 and 561 negative source readings, and KC1 has an instrument
transition. These conditions are reported explicitly in the manuscript.
`run_common_history_sensitivity.ps1` repeats the complete protocol using the
same 2021--2022 training history for all stations and writes a direct
common-minus-available-history comparison without overwriting the primary run.

## Figures

All PNG charts are generated by `src/pm25forecast/viz.py`. The architecture
diagram is the only conceptual figure that must be manually redrawn and
exported from Draw.io. Both `paper/figures/00_framework.drawio` and its PDF
export are retained.

## Remaining evidence boundary

The available-history confirmatory run is complete. A common-2021-history
sensitivity runner is supplied, but that result must only be discussed as
completed if `results/history_sensitivity_comparison.csv` exists. Inference
currently quantifies the hybrid--TimeMixer contrast; a future revision should
also retain paired predictions and compute uncertainty against the
station--horizon-specific strongest comparator.
